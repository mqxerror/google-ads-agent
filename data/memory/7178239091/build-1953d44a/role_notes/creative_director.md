## COPY STRATEGY RATIONALE
### Competitive differentiation baked into every RSA:
1. **"Official Government Partner"** — pinned H2 across all 5 RSAs. No competitor can claim this. It's the single highest-trust signal in the account.
2. **Named properties with pricing** ($307K Pullman, $347K Santa Maria) — competitors say "invest in real estate." We say "invest in Pullman Hotel." Tangibility converts.
3. **Tax-first headlines** ("0% Tax on Foreign Income") — every competitor buries this. For US investors paying capital gains and worldwide tax, this is a headline, not a footnote.
4. **"Plan B" emotional hooks** in every RSA — "Your Family's Plan B," "Protect What You've Built," "Secure Your Family's Future." Zero competitors are using emotional safety messaging. First-mover advantage.
5. **Anti-paper framing** — "Not a Paper Investment," "An Asset — Not Paperwork." Directly counters Henley's $100K (which is likely a fund/donation, not real estate).
