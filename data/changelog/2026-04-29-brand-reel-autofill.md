The Brand Reel form now has a **✨ Auto-fill scenes** button at the top. Type a one-line brief (or leave it empty if a campaign is selected) and the agent writes all 4 scenes for you — headline, subhead, stat + label, CTA, and the voiceover script.

**What it does:**
- Reads your campaign's pinned facts, recent decisions, and creative-director notes if a campaign is selected.
- Applies your brand rules automatically: USD currency, **no third-party brand names** in copy, no eligibility/qualification language, HNW-investor framing, program-specific positioning (Greece = real estate, Portugal = fund, Panama = QIV, EB-3 = USA green card).
- Refuses to invent CPA/CPC numbers for brand-new campaigns with no historical data.
- Returns ready-to-render copy in 3-8 seconds. Edit any field after if you want to tweak.

**Example output** (UK Greece campaign, brief = "Greece Golden Visa for UK retirees"):
- *Headline*: "Secure Your Retirement Across Europe"
- *Subhead*: "A Greek property investment grants EU residency for you and your family."
- *Stat*: "26 / Schengen Nations"
- *CTA*: "Book Your Private Consultation"
- *VO*: Brexit-aware 35-word script, brand-consistent.

**Press Enter** in the brief field to fire it, or click the button. Auto-fill overwrites prior field values — that's the point of the button.
