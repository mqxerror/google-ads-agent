title: Full-screen chat mode
tags: [chat, dx]
---

The chat panel now has a true **full-screen** toggle (the new expand icon next to the existing widen button in the chat toolbar). Click it to take the chat over the whole viewport — sidebar, content area, and memory panel all hide so you can focus on the conversation.

- Messages and the input are centered to a comfortable reading column on wide monitors.
- **Esc exits full screen** so you always have a way out.
- Resize handle and memory panel are hidden in full-screen since they have nothing to do.

The smaller "widen panel" button is still there for when you want extra room without losing the campaign view.
