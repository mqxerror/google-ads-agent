A new "Team" mode in the chat input lets you pose a question and have multiple specialist agents respond in sequence — each one shown with their own avatar, name, and color, building on what the previous specialist said.

Pick which specialists you want at the table (Director is always included), then send your question. Each role responds in their own card so it's easy to follow the discussion.

Confirmed approach matches BMad's "party mode" but adapted for the agency context — perfect for cross-functional questions like "should we increase budget for Greece?" where you want PPC, Search Term Hunter, Analytics, and the Director to weigh in together.
